Robert Bruce Banner Biography

Before The Hulk there was Dr.Robert Bruce Banner. Bruce is one of thee best nuclear physics around the world. During an expirament to replicate a super soldior using gama rays, he accidentally detonated a bomb. While it was going off he had to save the life of Rick Jones. Which is what ultimately lead to him getting exposed to gama rays and transforming into The Hulk.

The Hulk Biography

After the explosion and gain of an alter ego Bruce Banner became The Hulk. He is only occasionally capable of controlling it. The times he can't is when he is very mad and can't hold it in. Before The Hulk became The Hulk he had many connections with women, but once in The Hulk form he has a strong connection/love for a women named Betty Ross.
